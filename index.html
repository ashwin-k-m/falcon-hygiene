About Falcon Hygiene

Founded in 2022, Falcon Hygiene has grown from a small local manufacturer to a nationally recognized brand in cleaning and hygiene products. Our journey has been marked by innovation, quality, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

We specialize in manufacturing a wide range of cleaning products including disinfectants, sanitizers, detergents, and specialized cleaning solutions for various industries. Our products are formulated using eco-friendly ingredients without compromising on effectiveness.
